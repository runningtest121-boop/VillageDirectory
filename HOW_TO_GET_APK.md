# 📱 How to Get Your Village Directory APK (No Computer Needed!)

Follow these steps on your **mobile phone** — it's free and takes about 10 minutes.

---

## STEP 1 — Create a Free GitHub Account
1. Open your phone browser and go to: **https://github.com**
2. Tap **"Sign up"**
3. Enter your email, create a username and password
4. Verify your email

---

## STEP 2 — Create a New Repository
1. After logging in, tap the **"+"** icon (top right)
2. Tap **"New repository"**
3. Name it: `VillageDirectory`
4. Make sure it is set to **Public**
5. Tap **"Create repository"**

---

## STEP 3 — Upload the Project Files
1. On your new repository page, tap **"uploading an existing file"**
2. **Unzip** the `VillageDirectory_v2.zip` file on your phone
3. Select ALL files and folders inside and upload them
4. Scroll down and tap **"Commit changes"**

> 💡 Tip: Use a file manager app like "Files by Google" to unzip

---

## STEP 4 — Watch GitHub Build Your APK Automatically!
1. Tap the **"Actions"** tab on your repository page
2. You will see **"Build Village Directory APK"** running (yellow spinner)
3. Wait about **3–5 minutes** for it to finish
4. When it shows a **green checkmark ✅**, tap on it
5. Scroll down to **"Artifacts"**
6. Tap **"VillageDirectory-APK"** to download it
7. You will get a **zip file** — unzip it to find `app-debug.apk`

---

## STEP 5 — Install the APK on Your Phone
1. Open your phone **Settings**
2. Go to **Security** (or Privacy)
3. Enable **"Install from unknown sources"** (or "Install unknown apps")
4. Find the `app-debug.apk` file in your Downloads
5. Tap it and tap **"Install"**
6. Done! Open **"Village Directory"** from your app drawer 🏘
